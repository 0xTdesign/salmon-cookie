About Salmon Cookie

We make food using a few select high-quality ingredients – every one of which has a vital role to play in giving your dog a high quality, healthy natural food.

Why Salmon Cookie

  1. Grain Free: Our foods do not use grains – we prefer the better, but more expensive, carbohydrates, potato and pea
  2. No artificial preservatives: We use mixed natural tocopherols and rosemary
  3. No added sugars: Some dog foods add quantities of sugar to make their foods more palatable – we don’t, it's unhealthy and fish is already very palatable.
  4. No colourings
